Bombrix

Bombrix is a mobile puzzle game that is currently up on Google Play.
10,000~50,000 Downloads.

Bitmango - Puzzle Game Company Intern
June, 2017 ~ August, 2017

As a game designer I worked on designing the game, documenting all the details. The team I worked on did not have an artist so I also worked on the overall style and created the Icon for the game. On the process I created 240+ levels and leveled the arcade mode.

link to: Bombrix - Google Play

Objective

Clear the board by dropping the brick(s) to the right place. Three or more same touching blocks explodes!! Figure out a way to destroy all the bricks at once.
