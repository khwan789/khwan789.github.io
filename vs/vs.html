Versus Sketches


Fight my sketches!! Simple shoot 'em up game.


Personal Project - Game Design and Development
January, 2020 ~ Feburary, 2020
Made in Unity 3D developed in C#
Art Work - By Kyunghwan using Inkscape
Font - 'Timotheos' by Blooguer from Dafont.com (font for free use without restrictions)


link to: Versus Sketches - Google Play



Versus Sketches is a shoot 'em up game that I've made using Unity as a personal project.
I tried to focus on making a "simple" game and really took my time on deciding the line between what is simple and what is unfinished.
All artwork was done in Inkscape and since I am not a great artist I decided to go with the theme of sketches. I am planning on making a few additional casual games as a 'Versus Series' in the future using the same theme.
