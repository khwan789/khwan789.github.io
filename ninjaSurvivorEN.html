Ninja Survivors Online

'Ninja Survivors Online' is a mobile game in the survival RPG genre released in 2022, offering fast-paced, thrilling experiences with friends in an online environment, delivering the fun of roguelike gameplay.



Role Description

Chapter Planning

  • Created chapters with level designs that emphasize cooperative enjoyment and the thrill of defeating enemies, suited for a multiplayer survival game.
  • Designed over 100 regular chapters and trial modes for top-tier players, incorporating various map and stage gimmicks, along with boss patterns to refresh repetitive gameplay experiences.

Dungeon Planning

  • Designed daily dungeons where players of any growth level can freely enjoy and acquire the desired materials.
  • Developed boss content where four players cooperate to deal maximum damage to the boss, thereby enhancing multiplayer identity and earning more in-game rewards.





Chapter Improvement Planning

Original Chapter

  • As a multiplayer survival game, two players must cooperate on a limited-sized map to defeat incoming monsters and survive for a limited time.
  • Players level up by defeating monsters, and each level-up offers up to five skills to obtain and enhance.
  • Designed with a wave structure for short, focused gameplay, with chapters composed of six waves, each lasting around 1 minute.
  • Clearing the final wave (sixth) involves defeating a boss.

Background

  • Monsters spawn at random locations on the map, with restrictions to prevent them from spawning near the player's location, to avoid disrupting player movement.
  • However, players adopted a strategy of circling the map edges, focusing on survival, which led to a uniform gameplay style centered on equipment and skills that emphasized survival.
  • This allowed players with unintended specs to clear chapters by simply surviving the time limit, weakening the motivation for growth and rewards.

Improvement Directions



Monster Spawn Method Changes

  • Monsters now spawn in a circular pattern around the player, closing in from inside and outside the map.

Clear Condition Changes

  • Players must defeat a set number of monsters within the time limit.

Intent

  • Encourage players to move more dynamically, providing a variety of movement paths while promoting more active combat to achieve the required number of kills.


Rage System

  • When the time limit is reached, monsters enter a rage state.
  • In rage mode, monsters are enhanced and their movement speed increases.
  • Rage mode continues until the player dies or the clear condition is met.

Secret Technique System

  • A skill that can be activated at the player's discretion once the skill gauge, charged by defeating monsters, is fully filled.
  • This skill inflicts damage over a wide area and grants a temporary invincibility buff.

Intent

  • Provide intense pressure and thrill through enhanced monsters in rage mode.
  • Offer a broader and more varied gameplay experience by allowing players to utilize survival skills, including secret techniques, to clear the chapter.


Secret Technique Skills Matching Character Concepts




  • 'Luho,' a character who throws shurikens, summons clones around the character. The health and attack power of the clones increase in proportion to the player's stats, and the clones throw shurikens at nearby enemies.


  • 'Lucy,' a character who throws bombs, creates giant bombs that fall from the sky at a location designated by the player.


  • 'Kengun,' a character who wields a sword, rapidly swings the sword and charges multiple times in the direction controlled by the player.


  • 'Ryoko,' a character who shoots arrows, rapidly fires arrows in a fan shape. The character is fixed in place and rotates in the direction controlled by the player.
