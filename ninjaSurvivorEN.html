Ninja Survivors

'Ninja Survivors Online' is a survival RPG mobile game released in 2022, offering fast-paced and thrilling roguelike experiences with friends in an online environment.






Chapter Improvement Design

Background

Players with unintended specs could clear chapters by merely surviving the time limit, which diminished the motivation for growth and undermined the reward system.

Improvements


Monster Spawn Method

  • Monsters spawn more broadly around the player, with chapter maps expanded in size.
  • Players now respond to monsters coming from various directions in a larger space, requiring more strategic movement.
  • This change aims to break away from fixed survival strategies and encourage a more diverse range of playstyles.

Clear Condition

  • Players must defeat a set number of monsters within the time limit to clear the wave.
  • The required number kills increases with each wave (Wave 1 = 100 / Wave 2 = 300...).
  • Encouraged more active combat rather than a passive survival strategy to achieve the required number of kills.

Secret Technique System

  • A skill that can be activated at player's will once the skill gauge is fully charged by defeating monsters.
  • Inflicts damage over a wide area and grants a temporary invincibility buff, increasing strategic options.
  • Designed for each character, offering a new growth track as a character upgrade element.

Rage System

  • End of Time Limit : Monsters become enraged with increased strength and speed until the player either dies or fulfills the clear conditions.
  • Enhanced narrative elements and emphasized the importance of meeting objectives within the time limit.
  • Created an environment where diverse equipment and skills can be used to handle unpredictable situations.
  • Enhanced the sense of urgency and maximized the value of secret technique by allowing players to recover from challenging situations even after the time limit expires.

Secret Technique Skills Matching Character Concepts




  • 'Luho,' a character who throws shurikens, summons clones. The health and attack power of the clones increase in proportion to the player's stats, and throws shurikens at nearby enemies.


  • 'Lucy,' a character who throws bombs, creates giant bombs that fall from the sky at a location designated by the player.


  • 'Ken-kun,' a character who wields a sword, rapidly swings the sword and charges multiple times in the direction controlled by the player.


  • 'Ryoko,' a character who shoots arrows, rapidly fires arrows in a fan shape. The character is fixed in place and rotates in the direction controlled by the player.

Looking Back

Keep

  • Provided an environment where players could be more actively involved in the game's outcome.
  • Experienced significant changes through minimal adjustments within a limited environment.
    • Encouraged changes in playstyle through the adjustment of clear conditions and the introduction of the rage system.
    • Enhanced the Hack'n'Slash gameplay while maintaining the simplicity of a casual game with the addition of a single button for the secret technique skill.

Problem

  • Decreased player interaction in larger maps, reducing the multiplayer aspect of the game.
  • Difficulty in locating bosses in the expanded map led to automatically moving players to the boss at the start of the boss wave, disrupting the flow of the experience.

Try

  • A shrinking barrier gimmick over time to encourage interaction with other players and natural flow toward boss battles.
  • Implement following feature only in select chapters to add variability to gameplay.
