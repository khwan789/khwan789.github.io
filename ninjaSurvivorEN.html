Ninja Survivors Online

"Ninja Survival Online," released in 2022, is a mobile game in the survival RPG genre. It delivers the excitement of roguelike gameplay in an online environment, allowing players to enjoy fast-paced and thrilling experiences with friends.



Contribution

Chapter Design

  • Designed chapters that enhance the cooperative fun of multiplayer survival gameplay while delivering the satisfaction of defeating enemies through well-crafted level design.
  • Developed over 100 regular chapters and a "Trial Mode" that presents a strong challenge for high-level players, incorporating various map and stage mechanics, as well as unique boss patterns, to keep the gameplay experience fresh and engaging.

Dungeon Design

  • Created daily dungeons that allows players, regardless of their progression level, to freely enjoy the game and gather desired materials.
  • To strengthen the multiplayer identity, developed boss content where four players cooperate to deal as much damage as possible to the boss, earning greater in-game rewards based on their performance.





Chapter Improvement Design

Original Chapter

  • Two players must cooperate on a limited-size map to defeat waves of incoming monsters and survive within a set time limit.
  • Players can level up by defeating monsters, and with each level up, they can acquire and upgrade up to five skills.
  • The game is designed with an online multiplayer environment in mind, featuring a wave structure that allows for short, intense gameplay sessions.
  • Each chapter consists of six waves, each lasting about one minute, with the final wave featuring a boss that must be defeated to clear the chapter.

Background

  • Monsters spawn randomly on the map but are restricted from spawning near the players to prevent interruptions in their movement paths.
  • However, players adapted to this mechanic by repeatedly circling the map's edges during the time limit, leading to a preference for survival-focused equipments and skills, resulting in a more uniform gameplay experience.
  • This unintended playstyle allowed players with lower stats to clear chapters by merely surviving the time limit, reducing the motivation for growth and diminishing the reward experience.

Improvement Directions



Monster Spawn Method

  • Monsters now spawn in a circular pattern around the player, closing in from inside and outside the map.

Clear Condition

  • Players must defeat a set number of monsters within the time limit.

Intent

  • Encourage players to move more dynamically, providing a variety of movement paths while promoting more active combat to achieve the required number of kills.


Rage System

  • When the time limit is reached, monsters enter a rage state.
  • In rage state, monsters are enhanced and their movement speed increases.
  • Rage state continues until the player dies or the clear condition is met.

Secret Technique System

  • A skill that can be activated by the player once the skill gauge, charged by defeating monsters, is fully filled.
  • This skill inflicts damage over a wide area and grants a temporary invincibility buff.

Intent

  • Provide intense pressure and thrill through enhanced monsters in rage state.
  • Offer a broader and more varied gameplay experience by allowing players to utilize survival skills, including secret techniques, to clear the chapter.


Secret Technique Skills for Different Characters




  • 'Luho,' a character who throws shurikens, summons clones around the character. The health and attack damage of the clones increase in proportion to the player's stats, and the clones throw shurikens at nearby enemies.


  • 'Lucy,' a character who throws bombs, creates giant bombs that fall from the sky at a location designated by the player.


  • 'Ken-kun,' a character who wields a sword, rapidly swings the sword and charges multiple times in the direction controlled by the player.


  • 'Ryoko,' a character who shoots arrows, rapidly fires arrows in a fan shape. The character is fixed in place and rotates in the direction controlled by the player.
