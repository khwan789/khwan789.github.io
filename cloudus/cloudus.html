ABOUT PROJECTS

Cloudus 365

Cloudus 365 is a casual runner game with 360 degree rotation.

Class Project (Group of 3)
September, 2017 ~ December, 2017

I worked as the game designer, sound designer, and sub developer.
As a game designer I designed the whole game from start to end. I created all the sound on Ableton and participated on the development of the game.
At the end of the semester I took the game and connected it to google play and admob to publish.

link to: Cloudus 365 - Google Play

Objective

Avoid obstacles(black) and survive as long as possible. Tap to jump.
