Introduction

If you like games, then Coffee Pot is for you.

Coffee Pot is a game distribution and community platform. What is a community platform? It is a place where everyone can get together and share their favorite experiences. Whether you make mods, video reviews, mix tapes, or just leave a short review, you are part of a community of gamers. You can always go back to your Undertale subreddit, or your World of Warcraft forums, but on Coffee pot, you can say that you are a gamer, and mean it. Coffee Pot connects existing technologies through an all-device inclusive, native interface friendly, community focused application.

A coffee house is a place for meeting old friends and making new ones. The coffee pot is what holds the magic elixir that brings everyone together. So come on in: your coffee is ready.

My Part
