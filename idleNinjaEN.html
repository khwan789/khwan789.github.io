Idle Ninja Online

'Idle Ninja Online' is an Idle RPG mobile game released in 2021, popular among players for its fast-paced progression and unique system involving five shuriken decks. The game expanded further with the integration of blockchain technology for P2E (Play-to-Earn) features and the introduction of NFT pets.


With over three years of global service and consistent performance, it has established a significant presence in both the idle RPG and P2E game genres.

Boss Dungeon

"Enraged Shuten Doji"

Background

  • Developed a new dungeon for users entering the top-tier field, introducing "Ring" equipment to offer a new growth track and securing additional business model opportunities through equipment enhancement materials.
  • Strengthened the identity of the game as an online multiplayer experience by moving away from simple boss battles and creating a dungeon that emphasizes manual control and the importance of player cooperation, providing a fresh and engaging experience.

Content Description

  • Players face the main boss, 'Shuten Doji,' and the sub-boss, 'Ibaraki Doji,' along with their oni army.
  • Shuten Doji battles through powerful melee skills, while Ibaraki Doji supports with ranged attacks.
  • Utilizing the lover concept, Shuten Doji and Ibaraki Doji influence each other during battle.

Result

  • Secured 90-day PLC among high level users.

Shuten Doji

  • As long as Ibaraki Doji is alive, Shuten Doji cannot die. When his health reaches "0," he enters a stunned state.
  • If Ibaraki Doji dies, Shuten Doji enrages, entering phase 2, increasing his movement speed, skill range, and skill casting speed.
  • Players with sufficient durability but lacking firepower can prioritize eliminating Ibaraki Doji first, then dodge Shuten Doji's enraged skills while battling without Ibaraki's influence.

Ibaraki Doji

  • Ibaraki Doji can collaborate with Shutein Doji for the ultimate skill or also restore Shuten Doji's health during battle.
  • Skill: Creates 10 soul orbs, which heal the boss's health proportionally to the number of orbs remaining after a certain time. Players can touch the soul orbs to remove them at the cost of taking damage.
  • High-damage but fragile players can first neutralize Shuten Doji and cooperate with the team to disrupt Ibaraki Doji's skills and finish the battle.

Monster Tent

  • Destroying the monster tents placed in the dungeon summons onis to join the battle.
  • This provides an advantage to players using shurikens that excel at handling multiple enemies and emphasizes the need for party members proficient at dealing with mobs.

Retrospective

  • Ibaraki Doji's soul orbs were designed to spread out from their spawn points and to deal damage proportional to the player's health upon contact. The intention was for party members to share the damage among themselves. However, some players have been avoiding the orbs instead, or experiencing difficulties due to the increased difficulty when engaging in dungeon battles with a smaller number of players.

  • The soul orbs have been adjusted to remain fixed at their spawn locations, and the damage modified. The visibility has been improved to help players more easily identify the orbs' positions.





2024 Lunar New Year Event

"Rise of the Blue Dragon"

Event Theme

  • Event limited items are available during event period.
  • Event currency can be earned by completing missions that are achievable through gameplay.
  • Event limited items are categorized into various tiers, with the highest tier requiring currency obtained through mission completion and IAP.

Goals

  • During the event, a event limited Ultimate-tier pet is introduced to spark players' interest in collecting and offer a strong sense of growth for players at all levels.
  • The event focused on the game's first Ultimate-tier pet, "Blue Dragon," with content and business models designed to boost revenue.

Results


  • By giving early-stage players a chance to access powerful growth opportunities, the business model expanded beyond just high-level, high-spending players to motivate a wider audience.
  • Resulting into about double the revenue compared to a similar event held over the same 4-week period.

Blue Dragon Pet

  • Unlike previous pets that supported players with buff skills, "Blue Dragon" can both deal damage and apply debuffs to enemies, enabling more effective combat.
  • To further differentiate "Azure Dragon," it includes a skill enhancement feature and additional random stats upon summoning.
  • Random stats can be changed using items, giving players the opportunity to choose their preferred options, which also opens up additional business model opportunities.
  • Unlike other pets with fixed skills, "Blue Dragon" allows players to strengthen it according to their growth path, enabling players to customize its use based on their playstyle and goals, leading to further growth potential.

Event Progression

  • Previous pet growth: After reaching the maximum level, players needed to obtain two duplicates of the same summon and use a "Pet Soul Stone" item of the same grade to rank up.
  • To create a positive experience during the event, all players were given a rare-tier Blue Dragon for free.
  • Instead of requiring duplicate summons, the event introduced the "Dragon Pearl" item, which could be obtained through missions or purchases.
  • To enhance the value of the products and boost Blue Dragon's growth, "Blue Dragon EXP Potion," was added.
  • A dedicated event popup was created to facilitate the use of these new items and make Blue Dragon's growth more convenient.


Event Mission

  • By completing missions through gameplay, players can fill a gauge to earn "Dragon Pearls," the primary material needed for Blue Dragon's growth.
  • The event is designed to increase player engagement by ensuring that all players can experience the growth of their Blue Dragon.

Time Limited Offer

  • When the Blue Dragon reaches the maximum level (10), an offer is presented to provide the resources needed for the next tier's growth, encouraging players to make a purchase.

  • This design ensures that players feel a strong sense of satisfaction with their purchases as they experience the growth of their Blue Dragon through each tier increase.
