Idle Ninja Online

'Idle Ninja Online' is an Idle RPG mobile game released in 2021, popular among players for its fast-paced progression and unique system involving five shuriken decks. The game expanded further with the integration of blockchain technology for P2E (Play-to-Earn) features and the introduction of NFT summon.


With over three years of global service and consistent performance, it has established a significant presence in both the idle RPG and P2E game genres.

Boss Dungeon

"Enraged Shuten Doji"

Background

  • Developed a new dungeon for users entering the top-tier field, introducing "Ring" equipment to offer a new growth track and securing additional business model opportunities through equipment enhancement materials.
  • Strengthened the identity of the game as an online multiplayer experience by moving away from simple boss battles and creating a dungeon that emphasizes manual control and the importance of player cooperation, providing a fresh and engaging experience.

Content Description

  • Players face the main boss, 'Shuten Doji,' and the sub-boss, 'Ibaraki Doji,' along with their oni army.
  • Shuten Doji battles through powerful melee skills, while Ibaraki Doji supports with ranged attacks.
  • Utilizing the lover concept, Shuten Doji and Ibaraki Doji influence each other during battle.

Result

  • Secured 90-day PLC among high level users.

Shuten Doji

  • As long as Ibaraki Doji is alive, Shuten Doji cannot die. When his health reaches "0," he enters a stunned state.
  • If Ibaraki Doji dies, Shuten Doji enrages, entering phase 2, increasing his movement speed, skill range, and skill casting speed.
  • Players with sufficient durability but lacking firepower can prioritize eliminating Ibaraki Doji first, then dodge Shuten Doji's enraged skills while battling without Ibaraki's influence.

Ibaraki Doji

  • Ibaraki Doji can collaborate with Shutein Doji for the ultimate skill or also restore Shuten Doji's health during battle.
  • Skill: Creates 10 soul orbs, which heal the boss's health proportionally to the number of orbs remaining after a certain time. Players can touch the soul orbs to remove them at the cost of taking damage.
  • High-damage but fragile players can first neutralize Shuten Doji and cooperate with the team to disrupt Ibaraki Doji's skills and finish the battle.

Monster Tent

  • Destroying the monster tents placed in the dungeon summons onis to join the battle.
  • This provides an advantage to players using shurikens that excel at handling multiple enemies and emphasizes the need for party members proficient at dealing with mobs.

Looking Back

  • Ibaraki Doji's soul orbs were designed to spread out from their spawn points and to deal damage proportional to the player's health upon contact. The intention was for party members to share the damage among themselves. However, some players have been avoiding the orbs instead, or experiencing difficulties due to the increased difficulty when engaging in dungeon battles with a smaller number of players.

  • The soul orbs have been adjusted to remain fixed at their spawn locations, and the damage modified. The visibility has been improved to help players more easily identify the orbs' positions.





2024 Lunar New Year Event

"Rise of the Blue Dragon"

Event Theme

  • Event limited items are available only during event period.
  • Event rewards can be earned by completing missions that can be accomplished through gameplay.
  • Event items are categorized into tiers, and reaching the highest tier requires rewards obtained through mission completion and IAP.

Goals

  • The release of Ultimate-tier summon is will spark players' interest in collecting and offer a strong sense of growth for players at all levels.
  • The event is focused around the game's first Ultimate-tier summon, "Blue Dragon," with content and business models designed to boost revenue.

Results


  • By offering a powerful growth track even to early-stage players, we expanded our business model, that used to primarily target high-spending advanced players, to wider audience.
  • Resulting into doubling the revenue compared to the similar events held over the same 4-week period.

Blue Dragon Summon

  • Previously summons supported players through buff skills, but "Blue Dragon" introduces a more effective combat experience by applying both damage and debuffs to enemies.
  • To further differentiate "Blue Dragon," it includes features that allows skill enhancement and random additional stats upon summoning.
  • Summons have fixed skills, but "Blue Dragon" can be enhanced according to the player's strategy, enabling players to customize its use based on their playstyle and goals, leading to further growth benefits.

Event Progression

  • Previous Summon Growth: After reaching the maximum level, summons could be upgraded by obtaining two of the same summon and using a "Summon Soul Stone" of the same tier to increase their tier.
  • To create a positive experience during the event, all players were given a rare-tier Blue Dragon for free.
  • Instead of requiring duplicate summons, the event introduced the "Dragon Pearl" item, which could be obtained through missions or purchases.
  • To enhance the value of the purchased products and boost Blue Dragon's growth, "Blue Dragon EXP Potion," was introduced.
  • A special event popup will was created to provide convenience for using the new items and to support the growth of Blue Dragon.

Event Mission

  • At the beginning of the event, players will be guided to the mission popup, where they can obtain the Blue Dragon, introducing the mission system.
  • After acquiring the Blue Dragon, the reward will shift to "Dragon Pearl", the main growth material. As players complete missions the progress gauge will fill, allowing them to earn the "Dragon Pearl."
  • All players can experience the growth of their Blue Dragon, increasing immersion and engagement with the event.


Blue Dragon Popup

  1. Current tier, level, and name of the Blue Dragon.
  2. Allows players to view the summon effects.
  3. Allows players to view the effects of higher-tier Blue Dragon.
  4. Descriptions of Blue Dragon skill.
  5. Players can use items to increase the EXP.
  6. Displays the number of Dragon Pearls the player holds.
  7. Shows the materials required to upgrade to the next tier.
  8. Event Guide.


Ultimate Blue Dragon

  1. Randomly obtained additional effects can be changed to other random options.
  2. Blue Dragon's skills can be enhanced by unlocking the three available skills.
  • Players can unlock all three skills and switch between them as needed.
  • The upgrades require existing in-game materials allowing players to change effects and unlock skills even after the event ends, providing ongoing business model opportunities.

Time Limited Offer

  • To encourage purchase, when the Blue Dragon reaches the maximum level (10), players are presented with a time limited offer for the materials needed to upgrade the Blue Dragon to the next tier.
  • To achieve the highest tiers of "Divine" or "Ultimate," purchasing time limited offers and other event items is necessary, providing a clear spending guide for players.
  • To ensure that players feel a strong sense of satisfaction with their purchases the experience of growing their Blue Dragon through each tier is maximized.
