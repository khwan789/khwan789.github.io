Idle Ninja Online

'Idle Ninja Online' is an idle RPG mobile game released in 2021, loved by many players for its fast paced growth and unique system composed of five shurikens.


The game was able to reach further audience through the integration of blockchain for P2E features and the release of NFT pets.


With over three years of global service and steady performance, 'Idle Ninja Online' has established a significant presence in both the idle RPG and P2E game genres.


Role Description

Content Design

  • Field level design suitable for idle mobile games.
  • Designed Monster / boss patterns and implemented story elements.
  • Boss dungeon design where players can earn equipment and titles, providing a sense of growth and accomplishment.
  • The Four Gods : Early stage boss dungeons player encounters, designed with intuitive patterns.
  • High God "Enraged Shuten Doji" : Designed to challenge high-level players and provide new equipment for additional growth track.

Event Design

  • Seasonal events to drive player growth and in-game revenue.
  • Data analysis to identify player needs, improving metrics and driving revenue through designing event contents and BM.
  • Event items, offering additional stats, as attractive rewards for player motivation, and provides opportunities for additional BM through upgrade materials.
  • Summons (Pets) : Battles alongside players, offers growth potential through higher tier upgrades.
  • Costumes (Skins) : Express player individuality as cosmetic elements. Offers additional stats and effects through upgrade.



Boss Dungeon

"Enraged Shuten Doji"



Background

  • Targeting High-Level Players : Dungeon providing "Ring" equipment as a new growth track and additional BM products through upgrade materials.
  • New Experiences : Introduce new boss mechanics and features to provide high-tier players with fresh experiences.

Results

  • PLC : Secured a 90-day PLC and overcame growth stagnation with new equipment.
  • New Collections : Additional collections through equipment and boss defeat titles.
  • Community Enhancement : Official community, Discord channel, and in-game chats.
  • Strengthening BM : Additional usage for new and existing equipment upgrade materials.
  • Added usage for Ruby (paid currency) used for additional dungeon entries.

Dungeon Progress

  • Lover Concept : "Shuten Doji" and sub-boss "Ibaraki" influence each other during battle.
  • Shuten Doji : Uses powerful melee skills and charges toward player.
  • Ibaraki Doji : Supports the battle with ranged attacks.
  • Destroying monster tents placed around the map summons onis to join the fight.




Shuten Doji

  • Shuten Doji enters a stunned state when HP reaches "0" but does not die as long as Ibaraki Doji is alive.
  • If Ibaraki Doji dies, Shuten Doji is enraged and enters phase 2 : increasing movement speed, skill range, and skill casting speed.
  • Tanker-Type Players with Low Firepower : Eliminate Ibaraki first and then battle the enraged Shuten Doji, putting aside the unpredictable elements brought by Ibaraki.



Ibaraki Doji

  • Uses ultimate skills or skills to restore Shuten Doji's HP.
  • High-Damage Attack-Type Players : Neutralize Shuten Doji first, then cooperate with the team to disrupt Ibaraki Doji's skills and finish the battle.

  • Healing Skill
    • Creates soul orbs and heal the boss's HP after a certain time proportionally to the number of orbs remaining.
    • Players can remove the orbs by touching them at the cost of taking damage.



Monster Tent

  • Destroying the monster tents in the dungeon summons onis to join the battle.
  • Emphasizes the need for party members proficient at dealing with mobs.

Looking Back

Keep

  • Monster scripting for boss interactions, creating appropriate skills for battling against multiple bosses, and balancing difficulties.

Problem

  • Inadequate visibility, resulting in unclear information for the new features and gimmicks.
  • Gameplay experiences diverged from the original intent when first introduced.

  • Example : The soul orbs had high damage and spread out intended for players to share the damage among themselves.
    • Players avoided the orbs, and difficulty increased when fighting with fewer members.
    • To address this, soul orbs were fixed in place, damage was adjusted, and visibility improvements took place to help players identify the orbs' locations.

Try

  • Utilize UI design suitable for boss dungeons.
    • Deactivate unnecessary UI elements during combat and display information about the current state of monsters.
    • Provide explicit warnings or messages to guide players according to situations.





2024 Lunar New Year Event

"Rise, Blue Dragon"

Event Theme

  • Earn event items by completing missions.
  • Limited items available only during the event period.
  • The limited items are categorized into various tiers, with the highest tier requiring all mission completion and IAP to gain the event currency.

Goals

  • Game's First Ultimate-Tier Summon : Boost revenue through event content and business model design centered around the "Blue Dragon".
  • Spark players' desire for collection and provide a powerful sense of growth across various player levels.

*Blue Dragon Summon Tiers: [Rare] >> [Hero] >> [Legendary] >> [Divine] >> [Ultimate]


Blue Dragon

  • Effective Combat : Uses skills that deal damage and apply debuffs to enemies.
  • Additionals : Introduced summon's skill enhancement feature, and additional random summon stats.
  • Random Stats : Can be changed to other random stats, providing opportunity to select preferred options and creating additional BM potential.
  • Skill Enhancement : Enhanced according to the player's preference, allowing additional growth potential by choosing how to utilize the summon.

Event Progression

  • Positive Experience : All players received a Rare Blue Dragon during the event period.
  • Dragon Pearl : The event required the "Dragon Pearl" for Blue Dragon upgrade.
  • Blue Dragon EXP Potion : Used to increases Blue Dragon's EXP, provided throughout the event and IAP.
  • Event Pop-Up : Managing Blue Dragon's growth and using the newly introduced items.



Enhanced Skills



  1. A skill that deals low damage over a wide area, providing buffs to allies within the area, enhancing player survivability.
  2. A skill with limited range but increased damage to single targets, allowing players to deal significant damage to bosses.
  3. A skill where the projectile track monsters, efficiently eliminating enemies.

UI / UX







Data




  • Dragon Pearl : Applied a high discount rate to sell the event products.
  • Free Legendary Summon : Allowed players to experience the performance of the Legendary Blue Dragon and assess the value of Dragon Pearl.
  • Time-Limited Offers : The products were perceived as highly efficient despite the high price, stimulating desire to purchase.
  • Purchase Mission : Allowed players to earn Dragon Pearl points by purchasing products.
  • Preserving Dragon Pearl Value : Allowed players to exchange leftover Dragon Pearl for in-game items after the event ended.

Results




Revenue

  • $200K+ revenue in sales of event IAP.

Event Period Sales Products

  • Low-Cost / High-Efficiency Products : Shifted the BM focusing from high-spending players to a broader player base.
  • Achieved 32% of total event revenue.

  • Blue Dragon Products : Blue Dragon acted as a powerful growth track that appealed to all players, achieving 58% of total event revenue.
