Idle Ninja Online

'Idle Ninja Online' is a mobile game in the idle RPG genre released in 2021, loved by many players for its strong sense of growth and unique system composed of five shuriken decks. Later, it experienced further growth through the integration of blockchain for P2E features and the release of NFT pets.

With over three years of global service, it has established a significant presence in both the idle RPG and P2E game genres, based on steady performance.


Role Description

Field Planning

  • Implemented field level design suitable for idle mobile games.
  • Created monster/boss patterns and implemented storytelling elements to enhance player immersion.

Dungeon Planning

  • Planned and implemented boss dungeons that allow players to experience growth through equipment acquisition and the attainment of titles.
  • The Four Gods: The first boss dungeon players encounter, designed with intuitive patterns to reduce fatigue.
  • High-level boss "Enraged Shuten Doji": A boss dungeon challenging high-level players in a party format, strengthening the online game's identity while providing new equipment for an additional growth track.

Event Planning

  • Planned and implemented seasonal events to drive player growth and in-game revenue.
  • Utilized data analysis to identify player needs, improving metrics and driving revenue through content structure and business model design.
  • Planned and implemented event items that function as attractive rewards, offering additional stats to motivate players.
  • Summoned Pets: Participate in battles alongside players, offering growth potential through higher grades, providing opportunities for additional business models through required materials.
  • Costumes (Skins): As cosmetic elements, they express player individuality and offer additional stats and effects when enhanced, providing opportunities for additional business models through enhancement materials.

Weapon/Skill Planning

  • Planned and implemented the concepts of shurikens, the main weapon in 'Idle Ninja,' along with the mechanisms of new skills utilized by shurikens.





Boss Dungeon

"Enraged Shuten Doji"


Background

  • Developed a new dungeon for high-level players in the top fields, introducing "Ring" equipment to offer a new growth track and securing additional business model products through enhancement materials.
  • Strengthened the identity of an online multiplayer game, providing new experiences by deviating from traditional boss fights, emphasizing manual control and the importance of player cooperation.

Content Description

  • Players fight against the main boss "Shuten Doji" and the sub-boss "Ibaraki Doji," along with their goblin army.
  • Shuten Doji uses powerful melee skills, while Ibaraki Doji supports Shuten Doji with ranged attacks.
  • Utilizing the lover concept, Shuten Doji and Ibaraki Doji influence each other during battle.


Shuten Doji

  • As long as Ibaraki Doji is alive, Shuten Doji cannot die. When his health reaches "0," he enters a stunned state.
  • If Ibaraki Doji dies, Shuten Doji enters a rage, moving to phase 2, increasing his movement speed, skill range, and skill casting speed.
  • Players with sufficient durability but lacking firepower can prioritize eliminating Ibaraki Doji first, then dodge Shuten Doji's enraged skills while battling without Ibaraki's influence.


Ibaraki Doji

  • Ibaraki Doji can restore Shuten Doji's health by using her ultimate skills or disruptible skills.
  • Skill: Creates 10 soul orbs, which heal the boss's health proportionally to the number of orbs remaining after a certain time. Players can touch the soul orbs to remove them at the cost of taking damage.
  • High-damage but fragile players can first neutralize Shuten Doji and cooperate with the team to disrupt Ibaraki Doji's skills and finish the battle.


Monster Tent

  • Destroying the monster tents placed in the dungeon summons goblins to join the battle.
  • This provides an advantage to players using strategies that excel at handling multiple enemies or emphasizes the need for party members proficient at dealing with mobs in multiplayer mode.

Retrospective

  • Considering multiplayer, Ibaraki Doji's soul orbs were designed to spread widely from the spawn location, dealing percentage-based damage on contact to encourage players to share the damage. However, some players avoided the orbs, and the difficulty increased with fewer players, leading to challenges.
  • To address this, the orbs were fixed in place, damage was adjusted, and visibility was improved to allow players to prioritize their positions.





2024 Lunar New Year Event

"Rise, Blue Dragon"

Event Theme

  • Limited-time items available during the event period.
  • Event currency earned through missions that can be completed through gameplay.
  • The limited-time items are categorized into various grades, with the highest grade requiring mission completion and purchasing paid items to earn the event currency.

Goals

  • Stimulate players' desire to collect by releasing a transcendent grade summonable pet, "Blue Dragon," that is limited to the event period, offering a significant sense of growth to players of various levels.
  • Expect revenue growth through content and business model designs centered around the game's first transcendent grade summonable pet, "Blue Dragon."


*Blue Dragon Pet Grades: [Rare] >> [Heroic] >> [Legendary] >> [Divine] >> [Transcendent]



Blue Dragon Pet

  • Unlike previous pets that provided buffs, "Blue Dragon" can simultaneously deal damage and apply debuffs to enemies, making combat more efficient.
  • To further differentiate "Blue Dragon," we introduced a feature that allows players to enhance its skills, along with additional random stats that are applied upon summoning.
  • Random stats can be changed using an item, giving players the chance to select their preferred options, opening up additional revenue opportunities.
  • Unlike previous pets with fixed skills, "Blue Dragon" can be enhanced based on the player's growth direction, allowing players to choose how to best utilize it according to their preferences and goals.



  • A skill that deals low damage over a wide area, providing buffs to allies within the area, enhancing player survivability.


  • A skill with limited range but increased damage to a single target, allowing players to deal more significant damage to bosses.


  • A skill where projectiles track monsters, efficiently eliminating enemies.


Event Progression

  • Existing pet growth: After reaching the maximum level, pets can ascend by obtaining two duplicates of the same pet through gacha and using the "Pet Soulstone" item of the same grade.
  • To ensure a positive experience, all players receive a Rare grade Blue Dragon during the event period.
  • Instead of duplicates, players will need the "Yeouiju" item, obtainable through missions and paid item purchases, to ascend.
  • Increased item value and opportunities for additional purchases provide players with a clear progression path and ensure their engagement with the event content.



Time-Limited Offer Products

  • When the Blue Dragon reaches its maximum level (10), offer special items required for its next grade ascension, encouraging purchase through a well-designed experience.
  • To achieve the higher grades of 'Divine' or 'Transcendent,' the purchase of offer products and other event items is necessary, providing a clear monetization guideline.
  • Players will experience a strong sense of progression through the grade ascension of Blue Dragon, ensuring satisfaction with their purchases.



Results

  • This event provided a strong growth track even for early-stage players, shifting the business model focus from targeting only high-spending players to engaging a broader player base.
  • Achieved approximately twice the revenue of previous events conducted over the same period (4 weeks).
