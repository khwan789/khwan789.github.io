Idle Ninja Online

Game Designer

Idle Ninja Online is a mobile RPG currently available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.
As ninjas, players must grow strong to defeat the monsters that terrorize the villagers and the world.
I contributed as a Game Designer from the very beginning of development.
I designed systems, levels, content, and much more in the early stages of the development.
As the company grew, I mainly focused on content and event design.

• Ninjas throw shurikens and activate skills to damage the enemies.
• Ninjas can equip up to 5 shurikens based on their preferred combinations.
• I have contributed to designing the shurikens, from concept to skill mechanics.
• Worked with artists, programmers, and others to bring powerful shurikens live to the ninjas.

• Designed dungeons for early-stage equipment.
• Contributed from concept design to implementation of dungeon bosses and combat.
